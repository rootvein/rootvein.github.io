NAME: Haemaorcino ferox verrucosus ("Feeder")
HEIGHT: 90 cm - 210 cm
WEIGHT: 62 lbs - 196 lbs
HABITAT: Rooted deep into soil, mostly found around large agricultural areas.
DIET: Omnivorous. Mainly consists of living prey (human, monkey, deer).

==[TRAITS]==
Possesses a sense of sound, focusing on the vibrations of its nearby prey.
Has been documented to have a strong grip, allowing it to get its prey easier.
Only needs one living thing for the organism to use. This can last for a couple of weeks to a couple of months.
Connects the prey's DNA into it's root, causing the organism to grow into a deformed version of it's previous pray.

==[BEHAVIOR]==
Feeders tend to listen for sound vibrations for potential prey. It grows out from the soil to the prey, and grabs onto the prey with its roots. It prefers refers to get its prey in the fastest way possible.
After a feeder fully grows it's body, it usually becomes harmless, 

==[APPERANCE]==
The appearance of a feeder can vary: from looking like a human figure, to causing mutations to the body part they collected. Feeders with heavy mutations are rare to find, since feeders often construct their shape with great accuracy to the original organ related to its species.
